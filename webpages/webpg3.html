Dining

Woodbrook offers a variety of food options that cater to different tastes and preferences. Here are some of the must-try dishes in Woodbrook: 1. Ooty Varkey: Ooty Varkey is a popular snack that is made from flour, sugar, and butter. It is a crispy and savory snack that is perfect for tea-time. 2. Ooty Chocolate: Ooty is famous for its homemade chocolates that are made from locally sourced cocoa beans. You can buy these chocolates from the local shops and bakeries. 3. Ooty Biryani: Ooty Biryani is a delicious dish that is made from rice, meat, and spices. It is a must-try dish for meat lovers.
